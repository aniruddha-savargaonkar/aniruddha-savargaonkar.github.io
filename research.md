# Research

My research focuses on the biological evaluation of biomaterials and engineered surfaces, particularly how material properties influence bacterial response, blood compatibility, and stem-cell behavior.

## Research Themes

## 1. Biomaterial surface modification
Understanding how engineered surface properties influence biological interactions.

## 2. Bacterial interactions
Evaluating antibacterial performance and material-related effects on bacterial response.

## 3. Blood compatibility
Assessing hemocompatibility and host-relevant blood-material interactions.

## 4. Stem-cell and regenerative response
Studying how surfaces and coatings influence stem-cell behavior and regenerative potential.
