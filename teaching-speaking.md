# Teaching & Speaking

## Teaching

I have contributed to engineering education at Colorado State University as a Graduate Teaching Assistant in mechanical engineering design courses (MECH 201 and MECH 202), supporting large-enrollment courses through laboratory instruction and hands-on design learning. Additionally, as a Research Scientist, I have also substituted to teach a graduate level materials course (BIOM 531). My teaching and mentoring experience emphasizes experimental problem-solving, technical guidance, and student development in engineering contexts.
